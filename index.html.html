Stuff+ Model

Know exactly
where you stand.

Stuff+ is calculated from five physical inputs — velocity, induced vertical break, horizontal break, extension, and vertical approach angle. 100 = MLB average for that pitch type. Everything above is upside. Everything below is a development target.

VAA is estimated from your other inputs if you don't have it directly. The confidence indicator tells you how many inputs are driving the score.

About

"Built because this tool should exist at every level of the game."

Every pitcher has data. Most don't know what to do with it. Spin rates, movement profiles, and VAA numbers sitting in a Trackman export don't mean anything without a framework to interpret them and a clear direction for what to change.

The concepts being used at the professional level — Stuff+, movement profiling, platoon vulnerabilities — are not complicated. They just haven't been packaged in a way that's accessible to high school and college programs. This tool changes that.

Free, no login, works on any device. Import from Trackman or Rapsodo, or enter data manually. Turn numbers into a development plan.
